The Problem

Your website tells visitors
what exists. It doesn't help them plan.

  • Visitors leave without a plan

    Most destination websites do an excellent job showcasing what a place offers. But they stop short of helping visitors decide what to do, when to do it, and how to make the most of a short stay. Inspiration without a plan rarely converts to a booked trip.

  • Generic AI tools don't know your destination

    Tools like ChatGPT generate plausible-sounding itineraries that are frequently inaccurate. They recommend closed businesses, miss seasonal context, and overlook what makes your destination distinct. Visitors who encounter errors lose confidence before they arrive.

  • Building a custom solution isn't realistic

    A purpose-built AI trip planner requires meaningful development investment, ongoing technical maintenance, and specialized expertise most destination marketing teams don't have. Getaway handles all of it at a fraction of the cost.

3× more

Travelers who arrive with a detailed itinerary spend significantly more and are far more likely to extend their stay than those who plan on the fly.
